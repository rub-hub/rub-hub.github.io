About

We are a group of young scientists aiming to promote the unseminars culture in Roscoff as a tool to share our knowledge (and ignorance!), experiences and interests around biological research and open science. RUB is strongly inspired by Heidelberg Unseminars in Bioinformatics, as well as the Barcelona Unseminars in Bioinformatics, two forums committed to coordinate a meeting point for bioinformaticians to encourage networking in their community.

This project and this website are participant-driven, we encourage you to collaborate! You can do it through our github repositories.
